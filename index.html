README
ROAM
v1.0.0 · Personal Travel Intelligence

ROAM is a personal travel deal hunter PWA. It connects to real flight and accommodation APIs to surface the best prices for your next trip.

FEATURES
  • ✈ Real-time flight search via Kiwi Tequila
  • 🏠 Stays from Airbnb + Booking.com in parallel
  • 🔥 Last minute deals departing in 24hrs
  • 🌍 Inspire Me — cheapest destinations now
  • 👁 Price watchlist with 30/60/90 day history
  • 🤖 AI-powered natural language search
  • 📷 Real destination + property photos
  • 📦 All data stored locally on your device
TECH STACK
Flights: Kiwi Tequila API
Stays: Airbnb (RapidAPI) + Booking.com
Photos: Unsplash API
AI Search: Anthropic Claude
Backend: Cloudflare Workers
Storage: localStorage (device only)
GETTING STARTED

Follow the instructions in deploy.md to set up your Cloudflare Worker with your API keys. Once deployed, paste your Worker URL into Settings → Cloudflare Worker → Worker URL. No code editing required.

PRIVACY POLICY

Your data stays on your device. ROAM stores all personal preferences and watchlist data exclusively in your browser's localStorage. No data is sent to ROAM servers.

WHAT WE STORE (LOCALLY)
  • · Travel preferences (airport, pax, bedrooms)
  • · Watchlist items (flights + stays)
  • · Price history snapshots
  • · App settings (theme, display prefs)
THIRD PARTY SERVICES

Search queries are sent to our Cloudflare Worker, which relays them to Kiwi, Airbnb, Booking.com, Unsplash, and Anthropic APIs. These third parties have their own privacy policies.

Booking links are affiliate links. We may earn a small commission if you book through ROAM — at no extra cost to you.
